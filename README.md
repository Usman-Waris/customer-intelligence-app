🚀 Customer Segmentation Web App

A Machine Learning-powered web application that enables businesses to understand their customers better by grouping them into meaningful segments. This allows targeted campaigns, personalized offers, and strategic decision-making to maximize customer engagement and retention.

🚀 **Try the Live App:**  
👉 [Click here to launch the app](https://customer-intelligence-app-iezzhkvm7dqjbpgrunz98e.streamlit.app/)


🔹 Problem Statement

Businesses often face challenges in identifying different types of customers, leading to inefficient marketing campaigns and missed opportunities. Without clear segmentation, resources may be wasted on generic strategies that fail to engage key customer groups.

🔹 Solution

This application leverages K-Means Clustering to segment customers based on behavioral and demographic features. It classifies customers into actionable groups such as:

VIP Customers – High-value clients who drive maximum revenue
Time Pass Customers – Low engagement or casual users
High-Risk Customers – Users likely to churn, requiring retention campaigns

By segmenting customers, businesses can run targeted campaigns to retain valuable clients, convert casual users, and reduce churn.

🔹 Key Features

Customer Data Input: Upload CSV/Excel datasets of customer information
Automated Clustering: Applies K-Means algorithm to group customers dynamically
Segment Visualization: Interactive plots showing clusters and patterns
Actionable Insights: Helps businesses plan marketing campaigns tailored to each segment
Exportable Reports: Download segmented data for further analysis
